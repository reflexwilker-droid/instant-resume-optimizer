# Instant Resume Optimizer — $0 Marketing Strategy

**Goal:** Acquire first 1,000 visitors and 10 paying customers within 30 days with zero ad spend.

## 1. Reddit (Immediate, Free)

- r/resumes (900k+ members) — Post before/after examples (use generated ones). Title: "I built a free AI tool that rewrites your resume in 10 seconds — here are real results". Include link.
- r/careerguidance, r/jobs, r/techjobs — similar posts.
- **Rules:** No spam. Be genuinely helpful. Post examples with screenshots. Include "Feedback welcome" to encourage engagement.
- Timing: Post Tue-Wed-Thu mornings (US time) for peak visibility.

Expected: 500-2,000 visitors in first week if posts gain traction.

## 2. SEO (Weeks 2-8)

Target keywords:
- "resume optimizer free"
- "ATS resume checker"
- "AI resume rewrite"
- "improve my resume instantly"

**On-page SEO:**
- H1: "Instant Resume Optimizer – AI-Powered Resume Rewriting"
- Meta description: < 160 chars with keywords
- Alt text for images (if any)
- Clean URL: / (use custom domain later)

**Simple content:**
- Add a /tips page with resume advice (keyword gravy). Could be one page. This helps SEO.

## 3. Social Proof & Sharing

- After user gets optimized resume, show: "Share this before/after on LinkedIn/Twitter" with prefilled tweet: "Just optimized my resume with InstantResume.io — get yours free too! [link]". Use simple share buttons.
- Encouraging users to share = free virality.

## 4. Partnerships (Affiliate)

- Reach out to career coaches, resume writers on LinkedIn/Twitter. Offer them 20-30% recurring commission for referring customers.
- Provide them with a special referral link that sets a cookie.

## 5. Product Hunt (Optional)

- Launch on Product Hunt when ready. Even free tier can get 100-500 visitors minimum.
- Offer a limited-time discount code for PH users.

## 6. Email List (Lead Capture)

- Optional: require email for "3 free rewrites" already captures leads.
- After rewrite, ask: "Want more tips? Join our newsletter." (Use MailerLite free up to 1k subs).
- Nurture with resume tips and occasional upsell.

## 7. Community Presence

- Monitor Quora, Reddit, Twitter for "how to improve resume" questions. Offer to rewrite a snippet for free and link to tool. (Not overt self-promo, but helpful).

## 8. Repurpose Content

- Take before/after examples (anonymized) and turn into Twitter/LinkedIn carousels.
- Short video (Loom) showing how to use the tool.

## Budget Breakdown

- Domain: $12/year (instantresume.io or similar)
- Optional: Canva Pro for graphics? Use free.
- Total: $12, or $0 if using free subdomain.

## Conversion Estimates

- 1% of visitors become paying customers (industry avg for utility SaaS)
- 1,000 visitors → 10 customers → $150/month if they pick monthly plan.
- With scale, can improve conversion by adding more features.

## Key Metrics to Track

- Visitors (Google Analytics or Plausible)
- Free rewrites completed
- Conversion rate to payment
- Revenue

Set up a simple Google Sheet to log daily numbers.
